School Play Poster Project

(Play title: Pipeline)

A poster design for a school play in the City College of New York. The play is named Pipeline by Dominique Morisseau.
The crushed cigarette is supposed to represent the crushed hopes of the characters against systemic racism
as well as the corrupt education system. It is also a motif appearing throughout the play.
